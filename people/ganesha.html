Biographical Sketch

Ganesha is currently working with Samsung R&D Institute India - Bangalore as a Chief Engineer and responsible for certification of IoT devices. He has done his M. Tech in Computer Science and Engineering from NMAMIT Nitte (2009 – 2011) & B.E in Computer Science and Engineering from Vivekananada College of Engineering & Technology Puttur (2005 – 2009).

Prior to joining Samsung he has worked with Manipal Dot Net Pvt Ltd for 4 years on Algorithms development on Software configurable processors & development of Security Surveillance projects. He is having 9 years of diverse industry experience in IoT (Internet of Things), AI (Artificial Intelligence), HME (Health & Medical Equipments - MRI, CT & Ultrasound), Security Surveillance and Embedded Systems (DVRs, NVRs & IP Cameras) with over 5 years working in a world-class R&D center and nearly 4 years working in a highly entrepreneurial software and hardware design services organization. He is also having eexperience with Quality Assurance methodologies, processes, tools and testing strategies. Also he has contributed for 3 patents.

He is currently a part time PhD research scholar in IIIT – Bangalore sponsored by Samsung R&D Institute India – Bangalore.
