Research Interest : Formal specification and automated testing of web services, specifically RESTful Web services.

Biographical Sketch

Ms.Karthika is Technical Officer at C-DAC(Centre for Development of Advanced Computing). Bangalore, with 3+ years of experience in software research, design and development of e-Saadhya, an e-Learning system for children with Autism and Mild Mental Retardation.

She has done her M.E software engineering from College of Engineering Guindy,Anna University, Chennai. Her research interests are semantics, e-learning, augmented reality, ontology, natural language processing.

Her other interests are Drawing, Music, Singing.

Courses taken till now: Compiler Design, Automated Formal Verification, Theory of Computation
