Maithily Diwan

Degree Registered For: MS Research

Supervisor: Meenakshi D'Souza

Date of Joining: August 2015

Research Interest: Formal specification and verification of Embedded systems

Biographical Sketch

Maithily has over 9 years of experience in Embedded Software Design & Development. During her tenure at Robert Bosch she has worked in Active Safety Department of the Automotive domain.

She has led Engineering team for development of complete software project life cycle of Embedded systems like Antilock Braking Systems and Electronic Stability Program for various OEMs. She was involved in Development of Controller Area Network Protocol, Diagnostics and Application Software development using Embedded C.

Her areas of expertise are in field of Project Management, Software Requirements, Software Design, Verification and Validation of Embedded systems. Maithily has done her B.E. in Electronics and Communication Engineering from Gogte Institue of Technology, Belgaum. She is currently pursuing her MS in Software Engineering at IIIT-B.

Her other interests include Music, Dance, Yoga, Gardening and Painting.

Courses taken till now: Discrete Mathematics, Data Structures and Algorithm, Formal Specifications
