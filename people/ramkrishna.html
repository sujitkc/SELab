Research Interest: Software Architecture and Design, Systems Engineering, Lean Product Development

Biographical Sketch

Ramakrishnan Raman (Ramki) is currently working at Honeywell Technology Solutions, Bangalore. He has significant global experience in working with senior customers and stakeholders, applying thought leadership on engineering, strategic and operational dimensions. He has been the Lead Systems Engineer and Architect for the design and development of many complex systems in the domains of aerospace and industrial/ building automation control systems. He has championed many organizational initiatives and transformational programs - including Knowledge Based Development / Lean Product Development, Core Platforms/ Reuse Engineering and Systems Engineering Competency Development, with significant impact on product development cycle time and market responsiveness.

Ramki has B.Tech and MS(By Research) degrees from IIT Madras, and MBA from ICFAI University. He is also a certified Six Sigma Black Belt and INCOSE Certified Systems Engineering Professional. He is a Senior Member - IEEE, and Senior Member - Computer Society Of India.

Courses taken till now: Discrete Maths, Reading Elective (Operating System)
