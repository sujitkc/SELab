(Software) Engineering for Social Impact

Anandwan Project

In this project we are collaborating with Anandawan, a village near Warora, Maharashtra, which has about 2500 residents, most of whom are afflicted with leprosy, blindness, muteness or some other disability. The village is a landmark in a variety of aspects, like economic self-sufficiency, environmental sustainability etc.

In many of the issues faced by people in Anandwan and the various plans they have, advanced technology plays a central role. Accessible computing (for the physically disabled), IT-enabled health information system, technology-driven agriculture being some of them. We are collaborating with Anandwan to provide technological inputs to the solution to these problems.
